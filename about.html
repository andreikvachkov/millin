About us

We are a boutique women-owned company started in New York City. The city that never sleeps, a melting pot of culture, food, and music. A world’s fashion, business, and technology center. We created our luxury gift sets to reflect this dynamic and to enhance your senses with a carefully selected collection of garnishes. Our gift sets are perfect for all occasions, whether it’s personal, retail, or corporate environment.
What’s in the name?

The brand name “MILLIN” holds a deep significance in the context of the home bar and entertainment.

MILL = Embodies the transformative process where raw materials are skillfully crafted into valuable creations. It signifies that our gift sets consist of various components that, when combined, create an engaging and immersive activity, an experience that goes beyond the ordinary. Just as a mill skillfully transforms diverse elements into refined products, MILLIN takes these individual elements and merges them with precision, creating a cohesive and unforgettable whole.

IN  = Reflects the brand’s desire to invite people into our inner circle of home entertainment. It symbolizes the warmth, joy, and intimacy found within the realm of one’s home. MILLIN aims to create an atmosphere where individuals can come together, engage in activities, and forge unforgettable memories.

MILL+IN  = Is our commitment to transforming ordinary moments into extraordinary experiences. It conveys the MILLIN’s dedication to craftsmanship, the art of curating captivating activities, and the cultivation of cherished memories.
